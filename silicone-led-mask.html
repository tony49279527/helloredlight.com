Frequently Asked Questions About Silicone LED Face Mask

What wavelengths does the Silicone LED Face Mask use?

The mask combines 7 LED colors including 630nm (red), 660nm (deep red), and 850nm (near-infrared), plus blue, yellow, green, and cyan for comprehensive skin treatment.

Is this mask suitable for professional salon use?

Yes. The flexible silicone design conforms to any face shape, and the mask supports multiple treatment modes for different skin concerns. It is designed for high-volume professional use.
