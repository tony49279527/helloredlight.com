Case Study

How a European Beauty Salon Chain Standardized Red Light Therapy Across 8 Locations

15 panels deployed. One unified treatment protocol. Measurable client retention improvement.

Client Type
Mid-Market Beauty Chain
Deployment
15 panels across 8 locations
Configuration
630/660/850nm Triple-Wavelength

The Challenge

A European beauty chain with 8 locations across two countries wanted to standardize red light therapy as a core treatment across all locations. Before approaching us, each salon manager was sourcing equipment independently — different brands, different wavelengths, different treatment protocols. The result: inconsistent client experiences, no centralized training, and difficulty tracking ROI across locations.

Our Approach

Rather than selling them a single bulk order, we worked with their operations team to design a deployment plan:

  1. Pilot Phase: 2 panels deployed in their flagship location for 60 days. This allowed staff training, protocol refinement, and client feedback collection before scaling.
  2. Standardization: Based on pilot feedback, we configured all panels identically: 630/660/850nm triple-wavelength, white shell matching their salon aesthetic, branded packaging for the treatment rooms.
  3. Rollout: Remaining 13 panels deployed over 4 weeks, with each location receiving identical equipment and treatment protocol documentation.

Why They Chose Us

The operations director told us the deciding factor was our willingness to do a pilot order. "Other manufacturers wanted a 50-unit minimum. You let us test with 2 units first. That showed you cared about whether the equipment actually worked for us, not just about the purchase order."

Equipment Configuration

  • Pro-model panels with 630/660/850nm triple-wavelength LED matrix
  • White custom shell to match salon interior design
  • Adjustable floor stands for flexible treatment room placement
  • Pre-programmed treatment modes: 10-min (express facial), 20-min (full treatment), 30-min (intensive)
  • 3-year warranty with spare parts availability in EU warehouse

Results

After 6 months of full deployment across all 8 locations, the chain reported:

  • Red light therapy became the third most-booked treatment category after facials and massage — surpassing body treatments
  • Client retention rate for customers who tried red light therapy was significantly higher than customers who only received traditional treatments
  • Zero equipment failures across 15 units in the first 6 months of operation
  • Centralized training enabled by identical equipment across all locations reduced onboarding time for new aestheticians from 3 days to 1 day
