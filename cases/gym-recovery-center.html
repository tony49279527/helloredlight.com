Case Study

How a Mid-Size Gym Created a Premium Recovery Revenue Stream with Red Light Therapy

8 recovery pods deployed. A new premium membership tier. ROI achieved in under 12 months.

Client Type
Mid-Size Fitness Center
Deployment
8 panels (660/850nm)
ROI Timeline
Under 12 months

The Challenge

A mid-size fitness center in a competitive urban market was struggling with member retention. Their core membership ($39/month) was under pressure from budget gyms, and their premium tier lacked a compelling differentiator. Members wanted recovery solutions — foam rolling and stretching areas were crowded during peak hours — but the gym had no dedicated recovery equipment.

Our Recommendation

After evaluating their floor plan and member demographics, we recommended an 8-unit deployment of 660/850nm dual-wavelength panels, configured as dedicated recovery pods in a converted stretching area. The dual-wavelength configuration was chosen because this gym's audience — a mix of weightlifters, runners, and general fitness members — needed both anti-inflammatory (660nm) and deep tissue recovery (850nm) effects.

Why They Bought

"I was skeptical about whether members would pay extra for light therapy," the owner told us. "But after your team showed me the break-even math — 12 sessions per day at $15 each covers the equipment cost in under a year — it stopped being a risk and became a math problem."

Business Model

The gym created a new "Recovery Plus" membership tier at $89/month (vs. $39 basic and $59 standard). The premium tier included:

  • Unlimited red light therapy sessions (normally $15/session for basic members)
  • Priority booking for recovery pods during peak hours
  • Monthly recovery consultation with a trainer

Basic members could also purchase single sessions at $15 or a 10-pack at $120.

Equipment Configuration

  • Dual-wavelength panels: 660nm (60%) + 850nm (40%) LED ratio
  • Wall-mounted in private recovery pods with curtains for privacy
  • Digital timer control panels for self-service operation (no staff required during use)
  • 3-year warranty with manufacturer-direct support

Results After 12 Months

  • Equipment costs fully recovered through session revenue and membership upgrades
  • Premium membership tier conversion: approximately 25% of new members chose Recovery Plus within their first month
  • Recovery pods operated at roughly 65% utilization during peak hours, generating consistent daily revenue
  • Member satisfaction surveys showed recovery amenities as a top-3 reason for joining
  • Zero equipment downtime in the first year of operation
