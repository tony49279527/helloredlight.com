Why This Site Exists

The red light therapy equipment market is growing fast — but reliable, manufacturer-level information is scarce. Most online content is written by affiliates who have never seen a production line. We publish this site to share what we know: how equipment is actually made, how to evaluate suppliers, what certifications mean in practice, and which clinical claims the evidence actually supports.

Editorial and Review Standards

  • Health and performance claims must be supported by a directly linked source or clearly labeled as a manufacturer observation.
  • Regulatory status is model- and market-specific. Buyers should verify current documentation for the exact SKU and destination country.
  • Commercial relationships and first-party data are disclosed where they materially affect an article's conclusions.
