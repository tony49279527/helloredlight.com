Frequently Asked Questions About Pain Relief Therapy Belt

What areas can the Therapy Belt treat?

The flexible belt design wraps around the waist, back, knees, or shoulders for targeted red light therapy on joints and muscles.

How long should each session be?

Typical sessions are 10-20 minutes per area. The belt includes automatic timer settings.

Is the belt wireless?

Yes. The belt features a rechargeable battery for cord-free use during treatment.

Can it be used in a clinical setting?

Yes. The therapy belt is designed for both home and professional use with durable medical-grade materials.
