Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand site usage, and deliver a better browsing experience.

Cookies We Use

We only use two types of cookies on this website:

  • Essential Session Cookies — Required for the website to function properly, such as maintaining your browsing session and remembering your cookie consent preference.
  • Analytics Cookies — We use Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity to collect anonymous information about how visitors use our site, such as page views and site traffic patterns. This helps us improve our B2B website experience.

What We Don't Use

We do not use third-party advertising cookies, tracking cookies for behavioral advertising, or any cookies that collect personally identifiable information without your knowledge. Our website is a B2B manufacturing platform and does not serve advertisements.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, though this may affect certain website functionality. You can also adjust your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser data and revisiting our site to set new preferences through our cookie consent banner.
