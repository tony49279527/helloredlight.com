About Hello Red Light

Hello Red Light is a Guangzhou-based B2B red light therapy equipment manufacturer. Founded in 2020, our team brings over 10 years of combined experience in photobiomodulation device engineering, specializing in the design and production of clinical-grade red light therapy panels, targeted treatment devices, and full-body systems for professional use.

We serve a global network of clinics, salons, gyms, and distributors — providing factory-direct pricing, full OEM/ODM support, and internationally certified equipment backed by rigorous in-house quality control.
